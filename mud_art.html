About Mud Art

Mud Art, also known as Clay Art, is an ancient art form that uses natural clay to create beautiful and functional pieces. The process of making mud art involves molding the clay into various shapes and firing it to achieve a finished product. Each piece is unique and reflects the skill and creativity of the artist.
