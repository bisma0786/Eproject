About balochi handicraft

Balochi handicrafts showcase the vibrant cultural heritage of Balochistan through intricate embroidery, handwoven carpets, bold silver jewelry, decorative pottery, and camel skin art. Known for their detailed patterns and vibrant colors, these crafts reflect the skill and creativity of local artisans, especially women. From mirror-studded textiles to eco-friendly basketry, Balochi handicrafts blend tradition with utility, preserving the region's rich artistry and offering a unique cultural experience.
